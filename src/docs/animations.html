Animations

A handful of useful animations for fading in content. Make your website look even better than ever with this set of awesome animations.

Overview

Make your website pop with these cool animations thanks to Animate.css by Daniel Eden.

Below is a list of all the Animations which are stored within scss/base/_animations.scss.

Fade Animations
Class Outcome
.animation.fade-in Click to fade in
.animation.fade-in-down Click to fade in down
.animation.fade-in-down-big Click to fade in down big
.animation.fade-in-left Click to fade in left
.animation.fade-in-left-big Click to fade in left big
.animation.fade-in-right Click to fade in right
.animation.fade-in-right-big Click to fade in right big
.animation.fade-in-up Click to fade in up
.animation.fade-in-up-big Click to fade in up big
